Online Payments Setup
 
Follow these steps to get started with online payments:
 
Sign In To Verifone Central.
You will need your email address and password. Link to create your password is received in a separate email.
 
Find Your Organisation.
Navigate to Administration > Organisations > Search for your organisation > Go to your organisation's page.
 
Create Your Public Key.
Scroll down to the "Secure card capture" section and create a new public key. This is needed to securely capture online credit card data.
